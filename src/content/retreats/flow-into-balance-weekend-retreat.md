## Overview

Flow Into Balance is a nurturing weekend retreat along the shores of Upper Lough Erne, dedicated to grounding, alignment and emotional wellbeing.

The weekend includes yoga, Chakra Dancing, hypnotherapy-inspired guided relaxation, EFT, meditation, sound bath and mindfulness. Holistic therapy is available to book separately.

## What's Included

- Yoga
- Chakra Dancing
- Hypnotherapy-inspired guided relaxation
- EFT
- Meditation
- Sound Bath
- Mindfulness

## Pricing

The Flow Into Balance weekend retreat is listed at £260 / €300 per person.
