## Overview

Open to Joy is a gentle half-day retreat at The Lab in Cavan, inviting you to reconnect with lightness, gratitude and inner ease.

Through mindful practices, soothing meditation, gentle movement and a restorative sound bath, the afternoon offers space to breathe, soften and welcome more joy into your heart and daily life. No experience is required and all are welcome.

## Time

12.30 pm-4.30 pm on Sunday 23 August.

## Venue

The Lab, 65 Main St, Townparks, Cavan, H12 XC79.

## Pricing

€50 with vegetarian lunch included.

## Notes

This wellbeing day is listed in collaboration with Zensational Wellbeing.
