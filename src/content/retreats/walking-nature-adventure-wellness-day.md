## Overview

Give yourself a refreshing pause with Yoga, Walking & Adventure. This half-day experience invites you into a nurturing atmosphere of mindful movement, gentle yoga, scenic walking and reconnection with nature.

A beautiful way to breathe deeply, restore your energy and enjoy a sense of lightness and inner balance. No experience required. All welcome.
