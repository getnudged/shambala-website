## Overview

Soft Reset is a gentle Cavan wellbeing day designed to help you pause, breathe and return to yourself with more steadiness.

The afternoon includes a mindfulness workshop, mindfulness walking, Chakra Dancing, tea and coffee, and a vegetarian lunch. It is suitable for anyone who wants a calm, supportive reset without needing previous experience.

## Time

Please arrive by 12.15 pm. The day begins at 12.30 pm on Saturday 22 November.

## Venue

The Lab, 65 Main St, Townparks, Cavan, H12 XC79.

## Pricing

€50 with vegetarian lunch included.
