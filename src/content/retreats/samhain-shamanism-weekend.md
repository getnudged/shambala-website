## Overview

Join us for a soulful weekend of yoga, Chakra Dancing, shamanic practices, Cacao Ceremony, Fire Ceremony, sound bath meditation, nature connection and optional holistic therapies.

- Yoga
- Chakra Dancing
- Shamanic Workshop
- Cacao Ceremony
- Fire Ceremony
- Sound Bath Meditation
- Walk in the Fairy Woods

## Pricing

The Samhain & Shamanism weekend retreat is listed at £250 / €300 per person.

Therapy options are available at an additional cost. The poster also notes an optional Sunday island programme and visit to the Hare Krishna Temple.
