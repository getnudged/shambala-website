The Tai Chi & Yoga Weekend Retreat is a restorative residential retreat at Shambala Holistic & Retreat Centre with Fiona and Sukhada.

The weekend combines movement, rest and reflection through Hatha yoga, Restorative Reiki Yoga, Qigong, Hatha Vinyasa Flow and a cacao ceremony. It is designed as a quiet reset for guests who want a full weekend of practice, food, rest and time in the lakeside setting of Upper Lough Erne.

## Schedule

### Friday

- 6.00 pm: Dinner.
- 7.00 pm: Introduction and welcome.
- 7.30 pm: Restorative Reiki Yoga.
- 8.30 pm: Cacao ceremony.

### Saturday

- 9.00 am: Hatha yoga.
- 10.15 am: Breakfast.
- 11.00 am-3.00 pm: Free time and integration.
- 3.00 pm-6.00 pm: Afternoon immersion.
- 6.00 pm: Dinner.
- 7.00 pm: Chi Gong.

### Sunday

- 9.00 am: Hatha Vinyasa Flow.
- 10.15 am: Breakfast.
- 11.00 am: Closing relaxation and farewell.
