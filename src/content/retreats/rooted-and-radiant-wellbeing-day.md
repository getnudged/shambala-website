## Overview

Rooted & Radiant offers a nurturing half-day to help you feel grounded, centred and gently re-energised.

Through grounding techniques, mindfulness, meditation and Chakra Dancing, this experience supports inner balance, steady presence and the quiet radiance that grows from within. No experience is required and all are welcome.

## Time

12.30 pm-4.30 pm on Saturday 12 September.

## Venue

The Lab, 65 Main St, Townparks, Cavan, H12 XC79.

## Pricing

€50 with vegetarian lunch included.
